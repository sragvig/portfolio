Featured BuildThisIsFine — Girl Scout Gold Award

A free, web-based disaster preparedness game built in partnership with the Fort Bend County Office of Homeland Security & Emergency Management — featured on Fort Bend County's official Hurricane Resources page under "Test Your Knowledge." Six gamified disaster scenarios walk players through real response steps.

Coming from an area hit repeatedly by Harvey, Beryl, and the Kerr County floods, I built ThisIsFine to give young people a concrete way to engage with climate preparedness instead of fearing it.

Tabled at Back-to-School events with Fort Bend Emergency Management, spoke as an alum at Quail Valley Middle School, presented at the 2026 READY Fort Bend Expo, and shared the story on The Contribution Project's blog.
